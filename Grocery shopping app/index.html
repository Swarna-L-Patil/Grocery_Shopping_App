      How to Use the Smart Grocery Shopping Assistant

      Welcome to the Smart Grocery Shopping Assistant! This app is designed to help you create and manage your shopping list, track your spending, and get personalized product recommendations. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use the app:

      1. Create Your Shopping List

      Start by entering the name, quantity, and price of the items you want to purchase. You can add multiple items to your shopping list and view them in real-time.

      • Enter the item name (e.g., "Apple", "Milk", etc.).
      • Specify the quantity you need (e.g., "2" apples).
      • Set the price per unit of the item.
      • Click "Add Item" to add the item to your shopping list.

      2. View Your Shopping List

      Your added items will appear under the "Shopping List" section. You can review your items, their quantities, and their prices.

      • Each item will be listed with its quantity and total cost (quantity × price per unit).
      • The total cost of all your items is displayed at the bottom.

      3. Set Your Budget

      Enter your total budget in the "Budget" input field to track whether your spending exceeds your budget.

      • Once you add items, the app will automatically check if you're within your budget.
      • If your total cost exceeds your budget, you will be alerted with a red warning.

      4. Enable Recommendations

      If you want to receive product suggestions based on the items you add to your list, you can enable the recommendations feature.

      • Toggle the "Enable Recommendations" checkbox to turn on product suggestions.
      • Recommendations will appear below your shopping list based on the category of the item you add (e.g., fruits, vegetables, dairy, etc.).

      5. Switch Between Light and Dark Mode

      You can switch between light mode and dark mode for a better viewing experience based on your preference.

      • Toggle the "Switch to Dark Mode" checkbox in the settings to enable dark mode.
      • The app's background and text color will change to dark mode to reduce eye strain.

      That's it! You are now ready to start using the Smart Grocery Shopping Assistant and manage your grocery shopping efficiently.
